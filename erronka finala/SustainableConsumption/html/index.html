Urbanism and sustainability

Urbanism is and important and often overlooked subject when talking about sustainability. The way we organize our cities has a great impact in the emissions. Our goal is to spread awareness about this subject and educate people about subjects like car dependency, walkability, mass public transport and other subjects that are important to create a new and better world.

Car dependency

One of the biggest problems society needs to change is the over-dependency in cars and the high emission that comes with it.

To combat the over-dependency on cars and the high emissions that come with it, the sustainable urbanism movement advocates to changes in 2 main directions: Urban planning that relies less on the use of the car and instead allows people to walk or bike short distances to meet their needs and high quality public transport that allow for medium to high distance travel at a fraction of environmental impact that an individual car has.

Creating new cities

The center and perifery model for cities is deprecated and unsustainable. We advocate for medium density cities where people don't have to travel long distances(and therefore, use lots of energy) to meet their needs
