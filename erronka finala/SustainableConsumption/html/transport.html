Public transport

Public transport is one of the best ways we can combat polution generated in transport. With public transport we can improve the traffic, noise levels, polution levels and space of our cities. Check out the main public transport alternatives.

Train

Efficiency on rails

The number one method of transport when it comes to medium to long distance travel. The train offers unprecedented confort to those who ride it, while being extremely efficient energy-wise. From regional trains to cross-country high speed rail, the train has helped reduce the carbon emissions a huge degree and it will continue to do so while we build more train lines and faster, more capacity trains. Learn more about the awesomeness of trains in the following link

Bus

Adaptable and cheap

The bus offers a higly adaptable and ready to deploy public transport option in virtually any city or town where there are roads. Although not as efficient nor comfortable as the train, the bus is a great solution to transport people where rail lines cannot reach. They are also great when there is no infraestructure in place for better methods of transport, as a temporary solution while better alternatives are being built. It is also way cheaper than installing train lines, which makes it a very good options to deploy in zones where there is not sufficient invesment for better transport methods. Learn more about buses in the link bellow

Tram

Gliding through the city

The tram is a great middle ground to move the population inside the city while being as efficient as the train, since it goes on rails. It thrives in small distance transport as a way for people to move from one part of the city to another. It's simple and effective. It is also extremely comfortable and low on emissions.
